Places To Stay

Camp Ligma

The Campsite is located about 1.5 miles from the seafront, on Bulverton Road, B3176, and is open from noon on Wednesday 29th July, until noon on Saturday 8th August. Children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or adult guardian.

Camp Badonkadonk

Located at the Pima county fairgrounds in Tucson, Arizona, attractions are a plenty, combining music and art into a unique visual blend. About 50,000 campers will gather together with their canvas tents from different nations in this 3-day camping music festival event as the organizers are celebrating their 13th year.

Camp Giggidy

The camp will host legendary and multi-platinum rock icons like Metallica, Lynyrd Skynyrd, Godsmack, Staind, Papa Roach, Cypress Hill and a lot more for 3 days. The camp is equipped with shuttle rides, snack & beverage stalls, shower facilities, including, 24 hours medical and security staff.
